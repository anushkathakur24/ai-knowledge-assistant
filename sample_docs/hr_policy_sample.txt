EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — SAMPLE POLICY EXCERPTS (for demo purposes)

Leave Policy
Full-time employees receive 18 paid leave days per calendar year, accrued
  monthly at 1.5 days/month. Unused leave up to 6 days may be carried over
to the following year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 working
  days in advance through the HR portal.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per year. A medical certificate
is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

  Work From Home Policy
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
  Requests should be made at least 1 day in advance where possible.

Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses under Rs. 2,000 can be self-approved and submitted via the
expense portal with a receipt. Expenses above Rs. 2,000 require manager
sign-off before reimbursement.

Probation Period
New employees are on a 3-month probation period, reviewed at the 6-week
  and 12-week marks by their direct manager.

  Exit Process
Employees resigning must serve a 30-day notice period. Final settlement,
including any pending leave encashment, is processed within 45 days of
the last working day.
